# Platform Specs

Resolution, frame rate, and duration guidance per platform. Used by Stage 02 (script length) and Stage 04 (composition dimensions).

## Targets

| Platform | Resolution | FPS | Duration | Aspect |
|----------|-----------|-----|----------|--------|
| YouTube (long-form explainer) | 1920x1080 | 30 | 2-6 min | 16:9 |
| LinkedIn (organic post) | 1920x1080 | 30 | 60-180 s | 16:9 |
| Website embed | 1920x1080 | 30 | 60-300 s | 16:9 |
| Vimeo (client review) | 1920x1080 | 30 | any | 16:9 |
| Short-form (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) | 1080x1920 | 30 | 30-60 s | 9:16 |

## How to Use

Stage 02 keeps the script within the duration target. ElevenLabs cadence runs roughly 150-170 words per minute including pauses; budget the word count from that.

Stage 04 sets `width`, `height`, and `fps` on the Remotion composition to the values above.
